Help & Guide

What is a Tax Sharing Agreement?

A Tax Sharing Agreement (TSA) is required under Division 721 of the Income Tax Assessment Act 1997 to limit the joint and several liability of members of a consolidated group for the head company's tax liabilities.

Key Requirements

  • Must be in place before the due date of the relevant tax liability
  • Must reasonably allocate the group tax liability among members
  • Must be between the head company and one or more subsidiary members
  • Must be provided to the ATO within 14 days if requested
